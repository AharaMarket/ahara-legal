Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Ahara Hub ("we", "our", or "us") operates a restaurant inventory and team management platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Name and email address (collected at account creation)
  • Phone number (optional, collected to send operational text notifications)
  • Restaurant and inventory data you enter into the app
  • Device tokens for push notifications

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and provide the Ahara Hub service
  • To send operational text (SMS) and push notifications including stock alerts, order updates, and team communications — only to users who have provided their phone number and created an account
  • To improve the platform

SMS Notifications & Opt-In

Users opt in to SMS notifications by creating an Ahara Hub account and providing their phone number during the signup process. By submitting your phone number you consent to receive automated operational text messages from Ahara Hub. Message frequency varies based on your restaurant's activity. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out of SMS notifications, text STOP to the sending number or remove your phone number from your account profile. Text HELP for help.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We use Firebase (Google) for data storage and Twilio for SMS delivery. These providers process data solely to deliver the service.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us at support@ahara.app.

Contact

Questions? Email us at support@ahara.app.
